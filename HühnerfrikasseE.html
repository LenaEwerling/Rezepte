chicken fricassee

cookingtime: 140min
preparationtime: 30min
results in: 6-8 servings

If it has to be quick, change stewing hen with filet, cut it in pieces, roast gently and add.
  • 1 Stewing hen
  • 1/3 cup Butter
  • a little more tha 1/2 cup flour
  • 1 3/4 cups milk
  • 1 3/4 cups water from boiling the chicken/ water
  • 3 1/3 cups white mushrooms
  • 2 cups peas an carrots (already cooked)
  • 2 Tablespoons vegetable stock

  1. Cook the stewing hen about 2 hours until it´s done
  2. Let it cool down, pull the meat from the bones in smaller pieces
  3. Melt the butter in a pan, insert the flouer (it could result in a solid mass)
  4. Mix milk and water, give it in the pan step by step an stir very well
  5. Add meat, mushrooms, peas an carrots
  6. Spice with vegetable stock, stir well and let it cock for about 5 minutes
